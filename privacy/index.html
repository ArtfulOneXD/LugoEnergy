Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 1, 2025

1. Introduction

Lugo Energy Inc. (“we,” “our,” “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website lugoenergy.net or contact us through our online forms. By using our website, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information through our website forms or contact methods:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Address

We also collect non-personal data automatically through cookies, analytics, and tracking tools (such as Meta Pixel and Google Analytics).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To contact you in response to your inquiries or service requests
  • To prepare quotes or project estimates
  • To manage and improve our marketing and advertising performance
  • To maintain internal records and reports (including use of QuickBooks and Google Sheets)

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share information with trusted third parties strictly for business purposes, including:

  • QuickBooks (for invoicing and billing)
  • Google Sheets / Google Workspace (for internal project tracking and communication)
  • Meta Platforms (Facebook/Instagram) and Google Analytics (for marketing and performance tracking)

These partners process data according to their own privacy policies.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and tracking pixels to analyze performance and improve advertising relevance. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

We may use Meta Pixel and Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our site and to optimize ad performance. These tools may collect anonymous data such as device type, browser, IP address, and on-site activity.

6. Data Retention

We keep your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or as required by law.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

8. Your Privacy Rights (California Residents)

  • Request access to your personal information.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of having your data used for marketing or analytics.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@lugoenergy.com with the subject line “Privacy Request.”

9. Links to Other Websites

Our website may include links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites.

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
